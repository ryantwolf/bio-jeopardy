Inscructions

Singleplayer Goal: Get as many points as you can by answering the questions right.
Multiplayer Goal: Get more points than the other player/team.

  1. Click on a tile to get a question. Tiles are sorted by chapters and by how many points you will get for answering the question correctly.
  2. Type your answer into the box and hit enter to submit it. If you were correct, you will now get the points that were on the tile. If not, then you get no points.
  3. Click continue to procede, and repeat the steps. If you're playing multiplayer, it is now the other player's/team's turn.
